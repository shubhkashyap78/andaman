💑 Classic Romance – 4N / 5D

Explore Port Blair, Havelock & Neil Island with couple's spa and sunset cruise

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival – Port Blair

Airport pickup, hotel check-in. Visit Cellular Jail Sound & Light Show.

Day 2

Port Blair & Ross Island

Boat ride to Ross Island. Visit North Bay Island for coral viewing. Afternoon free for shopping.

Day 3

Havelock Island

Ferry to Havelock. Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach water sports (snorkeling + glass boat). Couple spa evening.

Day 4

Neil Island

Ferry to Neil Island. Natural Bridge visit. Bharatpur Beach snorkeling. Laxmanpur Beach sunset. Sunset cruise.

Day 5

Departure

Ferry back to Port Blair. Airport drop. Safe journey!
