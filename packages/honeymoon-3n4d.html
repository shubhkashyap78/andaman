💑 Starter Romance – 3N / 4D

Perfect short honeymoon getaway covering Port Blair and Havelock's best beaches

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival – Port Blair

Arrive at Veer Savarkar Airport. Transfer to hotel. Evening visit to Corbyn's Cove Beach. Welcome dinner for the couple.

Day 2

Port Blair Sightseeing

Cellular Jail visit + Sound & Light show. Samudrika Museum, Chatham Saw Mill. Evening shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar.

Day 3

Havelock Island – Radhanagar Beach

Morning ferry to Havelock (MV Makruzz). Beach time at Asia's finest Radhanagar Beach. Snorkeling / Glass bottom boat ride. Sunset candlelight dinner.

Day 4

Departure

Ferry back to Port Blair. Transfer to airport. Fond farewell with memories for a lifetime.
