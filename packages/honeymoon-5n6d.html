💑 Premium Romance – 5N / 6D

Luxury resorts, private beach access, scuba diving & island hopping across 3 beautiful islands

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival – Port Blair

VIP airport pickup, luxury hotel check-in, evening Corbyn's Cove Beach walk.

Day 2

Port Blair Heritage Tour

Cellular Jail, Anthropological Museum, Samudrika Museum. Evening S&L show.

Day 3

Havelock Island

Premium Makruzz ferry. Check-in luxury resort. Radhanagar Beach sunset. Private candlelight beach dinner.

Day 4

Havelock – Water Adventures

Couple scuba diving (certified instructors). Elephant Beach snorkeling. Afternoon couple photoshoot on beach.

Day 5

Neil Island

Morning ferry to Neil. Natural Bridge, Bharatpur Beach. Private boat ride. Sunset at Laxmanpur Beach.

Day 6

Departure

Return to Port Blair. Last minute shopping. Airport transfer.
